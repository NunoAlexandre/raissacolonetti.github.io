This project is a collection of 4 ebooks designed for the Kidiyo platform. The ebooks are a compilation of stories about the Kidiyo main characters. The narrative provides kids with information about the characters daily lives, who they are friends with and where they live.

Walter the Whale
Mick the Frog
Kelly the Crab
Beatrix the Bee

In order to see the full collection of ebooks check the Kidiyo app.

Storyteller: Benjamin Ryan
Illustrations: Raissa Colonetti
Company: Kidiyo
